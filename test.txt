This is a sample test study material about Artificial Intelligence.
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the intelligence of machines or software, as opposed to the intelligence of human beings.
AI applications include advanced web search engines, recommendation systems, understanding human speech, self-driving cars, and generative AI.
Machine Learning (ML) is a field of study in artificial intelligence concerned with the development and study of statistical algorithms that can learn from data and generalize to unseen data.
